Rules:
- Players must be registered to play.
- Player registration requires a nickname only; no personal data is collected.

- A new 5-letter daily word is selected at 00:01 each day.
- The previous day's game closes at 00:01 and cannot be continued.

- Only one daily word exists per day and all players receive the same word.
- Daily words must not be reused once played.

- Players may play one daily game per day but may resume that game across multiple sessions.
- Players have a maximum of 6 attempts to guess the daily word.
- If a player does not guess the word, the word is revealed at the end of the game.

- All guesses must be valid 5-letter words from the permitted word list.

- Player statistics are based only on completed daily games.

- The league table ranks players by:
  1) Number of words correctly guessed
  2) Average number of attempts (or percentage — choose one)
  3) Consecutive days of completed play (reset if a day is missed)

- A full archive of previously used daily words and their dates is retained and available to players.
