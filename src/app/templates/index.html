How to Play

Guess the daily word in 6 attempts. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.


The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was:

Green: The letter is in the correct spot.
Yellow: The letter is in the word but wrong spot.
Grey: The letter is not in the word.

Rules:

  • Only one daily word exists per day. All family members get the same word.
  • A new game commences daily at 00:01 Europe/London timezone.
  • Streaks are calculated for consecutive completed games. Missing a day resets it!
